Welcome to Duracrete Limited

'Duracrete Limited is one of the first Indigenous companies that pioneered the production of bagged/canned premium grade Decorative concrete products since 2012 here in Nigeria. In an era characterized by dominance of imported products in our market. Our products are Formulated in Turkey and Premium Raw materials sourced from Germany,Turkey and Nigeria.

Our mission is to promote Decorative Concrete Flooring Systems in Nigeria by offering premium grade "Nigerian Made" products that are available in limitless colors and yet affordable. We specifically make for our climate. We are skilled professionals in Industrial Chemistry, Geology, Material Technology and Landscape Architecture.

We love what we do and we believe in creating real jobs in Nigeria. Please call us for a free project material quote. Duracrete Limited is a subsidiary company of Durabuild Industries Group.'
